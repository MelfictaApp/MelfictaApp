What is
Melficta?

Melficta is a free download app that allows you to explore and get to know the history of the most suggestive place in Molfetta, situated North from Bari, city known for its sea and its history.

Image Description

Why did we
created this app?

The app comes from the necessities to allow tourists, and of course residents, an easily legible, modern -and most important- free service.
We want it to be easily readable because many websites and the few competing apps use a dated, unclear, and non-intuitive design.
Also, the app is free because it allows us to extol the history of our city in a simple way through the smartphone. 

Image Description

Who are we
trying to reach?

The app is not developed only for tourist, letting them have a preview of what will wait form them once they are in Molfetta, but it was also developed thinking about the more curious residents that wants to discover some less-known facts about the city.  

Video Description

What does the
logo stand for?

The logo takes inspiration from the most well-known architectural element, which is the “Duomo Vecchio di San Corrado”, situated on the old touristic harbor. It also displays the letter M and L, letter which can be found inside the old Latin name of the city.

Image Description

Why did we choose
these colors?

We choose the blue cause it represents the Adriatic Sea that bathes Molfetta and from which we sustain. Then, we choose red because it symbolizes the story of the city and as well, it represents the color of the coat from the statue of “Madonna dei Martiri”, patroness of sailors and symbol of the city itself.
